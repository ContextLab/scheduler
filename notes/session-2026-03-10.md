### Key Architecture Decisions
- Google Apps Script as free backend (no paid services)
- CORS workaround: text/plain content-type + redirect:follow
- Google Sheets as booking store with LockService for concurrency
- CalendarApp native invites + MailApp iCal for non-Google users
- Availability windows identified by title pattern match (not free/busy status)
- Token expiry: 90 days from booking creation
